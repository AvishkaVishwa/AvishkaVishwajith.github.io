RoboCore - Custom Robotics PCB

Custom-designed robotics PCB platform with modular power and control sections for robot builds.

PCBRoboticsPowerControl

Overview

RoboCore is a custom PCB designed to serve as a reusable base for robotics projects, focusing on clean power delivery and modular IO.

Goals

  • Modular headers for sensors and actuators
  • Stable power rails for motors and logic
  • Compact layout for enclosure-ready builds

Hardware

  • MCU section with programming header
  • Motor driver footprint or connector
  • Sensor and expansion headers

Power Architecture

  • Dedicated motor power input
  • Regulated 5V and 3.3V rails
  • Protection and filtering for noise reduction

Layout Notes

  • Short motor current paths
  • Ground separation for power and logic
  • Silkscreen labeling for fast assembly
